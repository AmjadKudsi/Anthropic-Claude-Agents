## Cultural Do's and Don'ts

**Essential Politeness:**
- Say "Bonjour" entering any shop/restaurant and "Au revoir" when leaving
- Learn basic phrases - even poor French is appreciated
- Dress nicely; presentation matters in France

**Dining Basics:**
- Make restaurant reservations when possible
- Don't rush meals - take your time
- Keep hands visible on the table
- Tipping is optional (service included in bill)

**Social Norms:**
- Use formal "vous" unless invited to use "tu"
- Speak quietly in public
- Don't expect strangers to smile back on the street
